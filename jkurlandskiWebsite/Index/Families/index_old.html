Greetings

Welcome to Adventures in Genealogy, which tracks my discoveries and missteps as I try to shed some light on my ancestors.

Here I cover branches of the following St. Louis-based clans:

  • The Kurlandskis: my father's father's side
  • The Nawrockis: my father's mother's side
  • The AuBuchons: my mother's father's side

I'll also touch upon the following families:

  • The Knoppes: related to the AuBuchons
  • The Kanias: related to the Nawrockis
  • The Wroblewskis: related to the Kurlandskis

Start by clicking on one of the links above or at right.

Please keep in mind that this is a work in progress. I make no claim that this material is complete or error-free. In fact, I'm eager to learn of any corrections or additional information you might have.
